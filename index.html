About

I am an IT Intern at Orrick, Herrington, & Sutcliffe LLP. As a part of their Applications Deployment team, I assist in imaging, configuring, and managing the firm's Macs. On a typical workday, I find myself writing a BASH script, helping to resolve a user's problem, and writing documentation.

In May 2019, I graduated from Franciscan University of Steubenville with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Science. While at Franciscan, I took various computer science classes, but I particularly enjoyed the Web Page / Creative Cloud and Hardware and Software Systems courses. During my senior year, I started Franciscan's 4 plus 1 MBA program, which I completed in May 2020. This program allowed me to work on graduate business courses while I finished my degree in CIS.

I was raised in a small town in Ohio, but I was born in Pittsburgh, PA. As a result, I am a fan of the Pittsburgh Penguins and love spending time in the Pittsburgh area. I am also a dog lover. My family has had three Golden Retrievers. We now have another Golden and a German Shepherd. In my free time, I enjoy cooking, baking, reading, photography, playing video games, spending time with family and friends, and taking care of our dogs. I also enjoy learning more about Macs and web development.

I strive to live by the following words from Walt Disney: keep moving forward.
